BURTIVILLE B18

BURTIVILLE B18 was a project that allowed for a deep exploration of form and materiality. The design is a conversation between bold geometric shapes and the subtle textures of natural materials. The goal was to create a building that is not just a structure, but a sculpture that interacts with its environment. The play of light and shadow on the facade creates a dynamic experience that changes throughout the day. This project was a labor of love, and I am incredibly proud of the way it pushes the boundaries of contemporary design.
