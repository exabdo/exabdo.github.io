NIC - MARINA ROYAL VILLA

The NIC - MARINA ROYAL VILLA project was an extraordinary opportunity to design a residential masterpiece that truly embodies luxury and exclusivity. Situated on Al Kasir Island, this development offers unparalleled waterfront living with breathtaking views of the Arabian Gulf. My design approach focused on creating expansive, light-filled spaces that seamlessly connect with the stunning outdoor environment. Every detail, from the selection of premium materials to the integration of smart home technologies, was meticulously curated to provide residents with an experience of ultimate comfort and sophistication. It was an immense pleasure to contribute to a project that sets a new benchmark for opulent living in Abu Dhabi.
