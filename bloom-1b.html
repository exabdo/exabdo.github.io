BLOOM 1B

This project was a journey in crafting homes that are both elegant and functional. The design philosophy for BLOOM 1B was to create spaces that inspire a sense of tranquility and harmony. Every line and every curve was meticulously planned to maximize natural light and create a seamless flow between the indoor and outdoor environments. Working on this project was a rewarding experience, as it allowed me to explore the intersection of modern design and comfortable living. The result is a collection of residences that are not just beautiful, but also a joy to live in.
